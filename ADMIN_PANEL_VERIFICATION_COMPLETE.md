## 📝 **How to Use**

### **Keyboard Shortcuts**
Press `?` anywhere in the admin panel to see all available shortcuts.

### **Quick Navigation**
Use `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + [letter]` to quickly navigate to:
- `D` - Dashboard
- `O` - Orders
- `P` - Products
- `U` - Users
- `C` - Couriers
- `M` - Live Map
- `L` - Live Orders
- `A` - Analytics
- `S` - Settings

### **Search**
Press `Cmd/Ctrl + K` to open global search.
